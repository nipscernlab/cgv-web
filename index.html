Drag and drop an ATLAS collision XML onto the viewport, or click to browse. Each <cell> needs l (layer 0–25), eta (signed integer), phi (index), and e (energy in MeV). Positive eta maps to Side A (+Z), negative to Side C (−Z).

High-energy cells emit a neon bloom effect. Geometry respects the barrel/endcap crack at |η| ≈ 1.5. Up to ~200 000 cells use instanced meshes for real-time performance.
